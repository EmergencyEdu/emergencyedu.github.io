EmergencyEdu

The app You're about to download is a knowledge base for international students. It's an actual collection of information about the rules of law established in Poland. Furthermore, it helps in crisis situations (e.g theft, accident, medical assistance). Its aim is to help with integration of foreigners, especially the newcommers. It was designed by a student of the Academy of Fine Arts and programmed by students of the University of Science and Technology in Wroclaw.
